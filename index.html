General
Who is behind jinsei.vc?

Three seasoned executives from the medical device, pharmaceutical, and finance industries. If you want to find out more, please visit our “About us” page.

What is your investment thesis?

We identify teams that can execute on transformative innovations with potential impact to help the lives of millions. For more information, please visit our “Investments” page.

Can I join your team?

Not yet. But we might be expanding our team very soon. Stay tuned!.

Why Boston-Silicon Valley-Tokyo?

Boston is the main biotech innovation centers in the world. It is also one of the main hubs for medtech, with top universities, hospitals, and research institutes. Silicon Valley is the main technology innovation area in the world. Japan is home of some of the largest medical device and technology companies in the world, most of them headquartered in or near Tokyo.

Are you affiliated with Harvard, MIT, and Stanford?

Our founders hold different official positions in those universities. But more importantly, they interact daily with their innovators. Nevertheless, please note jinsei.vc is not officially affiliated with any other entity.

Why is this website missing key information?

We believe in constant iteration and improvement. We believe in transparency. We prefer to publish an unfinished website, and keep on building and expanding, than waiting until every single piece of information is ready to be disclosed.
